Venezuelan Culture

American influence in Venezuela is really important. We as a nation have so many things in common with american people

Venezuelan Religion

The majority of Venezuelans are Christians because of the heritage received by the Spanish conquerors. Although also immigrants from other countries broght their religion with them as for example Islamism

Venezuelan Language

In the same way we received our religion from the spanish conquerors, spanish is our official language but Venezuelans also have a big amount of native languages alive in some of the indigenous population

Venezuelan Music

Venezuelan music is a mixture of different cultures, using genres from african descendents, natives and spanish. In my case I used to hear Salsa and Merengue!
